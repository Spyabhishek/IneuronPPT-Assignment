Q.4 Explain OOPS ? 

Ans - OOPS stands for Object-Oriented Programming Paradigm. It is a programming paradigm that organizes software design around objects, which are instances of classes. OOPS focuses on modeling real-world entities as objects and defining their behavior through classes and their interactions.

There are 4 main principles of OOPS:
- Encapsulation
- Inheritance
- Polymorphism
- Abstraction
