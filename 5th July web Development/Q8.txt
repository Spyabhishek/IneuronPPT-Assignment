Q.8 What is the difference between <figure> tag and <img> tag?

Ans. - 
<img> tag:

- The <img> tag is a self-closing tag used to insert an image into an HTML document. It does not require a closing tag.
- It is used to define an image element on a webpage.
- Ex : <img src="image.jpg" alt="An image">

<figure> tag:

- The <figure> tag is used to group and provide a semantic meaning to a self-contained piece of content, typically an image or an illustration, along with its associated caption or description. 
- It is used to define a self-contained figure or illustration within an HTML document.
- Ex: 
<figure>
  <img src="image.jpg" alt="image">
  <figcaption>This is a caption for the image.</figcaption>
</figure>
